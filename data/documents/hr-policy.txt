SKYNIXA HUMAN RESOURCES POLICY HANDBOOK
Last Updated: January 2025

1. WORK HOURS
Standard work hours at SkyNixa are Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM local time.
Flexible start times between 8:00 AM and 10:00 AM are permitted with manager approval.

2. PAID TIME OFF (PTO)
- New employees receive 15 PTO days per year, accrued monthly.
- After 3 years of service, PTO increases to 20 days per year.
- After 5 years of service, PTO increases to 25 days per year.
- PTO requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance via the HR portal.
- Unused PTO rolls over up to 5 days into the next calendar year.

3. SICK LEAVE
Employees receive 10 paid sick days per year. Sick leave does not roll over.
For absences longer than 3 consecutive days, a doctor's note may be required.

4. PARENTAL LEAVE
SkyNixa offers 12 weeks of paid parental leave for all new parents (birth, adoption, or foster).
An additional 4 weeks of unpaid leave is available under FMLA guidelines.

5. CODE OF CONDUCT
All employees must treat colleagues with respect, maintain confidentiality of company information,
and report conflicts of interest to HR. Harassment and discrimination are strictly prohibited.

6. PERFORMANCE REVIEWS
Annual performance reviews are conducted in Q4. Mid-year check-ins occur in June.
Employees are eligible for merit-based raises following their first full year of employment.

7. TERMINATION & RESIGNATION
Employees must provide at least 2 weeks written notice when resigning.
SkyNixa may terminate employment at-will in accordance with applicable labor laws.
