SKYNIXA NEW EMPLOYEE ONBOARDING GUIDE
Welcome to SkyNixa!

DAY 1 — ORIENTATION
- Arrive at 9:00 AM at the main office reception (or join the virtual orientation Zoom link)
- Meet your onboarding buddy assigned by HR
- Complete I-9 and tax paperwork with HR
- Receive company laptop, badge, and welcome kit
- Attend the "Welcome to SkyNixa" session (company history, values, org structure)
- Set up email, Slack, and HR portal accounts with IT support

WEEK 1 — GETTING STARTED
- Complete mandatory compliance training in the learning portal (due by Day 5)
- Meet with your direct manager for role expectations and 30-60-90 day plan
- Schedule 1:1 introductions with key team members
- Review department-specific documentation in the internal wiki
- Enroll in benefits during your first 30 days (see benefits-overview.txt)

WEEK 2-4 — RAMP UP
- Attend team standups and department meetings
- Complete role-specific technical onboarding modules
- Submit your first weekly status update to your manager
- Join relevant Slack channels (#general, #your-team, #random)
- Schedule a 30-day check-in with HR

REQUIRED TRAINING (First 30 Days)
1. Security Awareness Training
2. Anti-Harassment & DEI Training
3. Data Privacy & GDPR Basics
4. Code of Conduct Acknowledgment

KEY CONTACTS
- HR: hr@skynixa.com
- IT Help Desk: it-help@skynixa.com (ext. 200)
- Your Manager: listed in the HR portal
- Onboarding Buddy: assigned on Day 1

QUESTIONS?
Visit the #new-hires Slack channel or email onboarding@skynixa.com
