# SkyNixa IT & Security Policy

**Effective Date:** January 2025  
**Owner:** IT Security Team (security@skynixa.com)

## Password Requirements

All SkyNixa systems require strong passwords that meet the following criteria:

- Minimum **12 characters** in length
- At least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, one number, and one special character
- Passwords must be changed every **90 days**
- Do not reuse the last **5 passwords**
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is **mandatory** for all employees

## Acceptable Use

Company devices and networks are for business purposes. Limited personal use is permitted
during breaks. The following are **prohibited**:

- Installing unauthorized software
- Sharing credentials with anyone, including colleagues
- Storing company data on personal cloud services (Dropbox, personal Google Drive, etc.)
- Accessing inappropriate or illegal content

## Data Classification

| Level | Description | Examples |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| Public | Safe to share externally | Marketing materials, job postings |
| Internal | SkyNixa employees only | Meeting notes, org charts |
| Confidential | Restricted access | Financial reports, customer data |
| Restricted | Highest sensitivity | Encryption keys, executive compensation |

## Remote Access

Employees working remotely must:

- Use the company VPN for all internal system access
- Keep operating systems and antivirus up to date
- Lock screens when stepping away (auto-lock after 5 minutes)
- Never use public Wi-Fi without VPN

## Incident Reporting

Report security incidents immediately to security@skynixa.com or call the IT hotline at ext. 911.
Do not attempt to investigate security breaches on your own.

## Device Policy

- Company-issued laptops must have full-disk encryption enabled
- Lost or stolen devices must be reported within **1 hour**
- Personal devices may not access confidential or restricted data
