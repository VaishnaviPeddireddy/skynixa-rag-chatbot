SKYNIXA COMPANY OVERVIEW
Last Updated: January 2025

ABOUT SKYNIXA
SkyNixa is a cloud software company that builds tools for modern teams — project management,
internal communications, and employee experience platforms. Founded in 2018, we serve mid-size
and enterprise customers across North America and Europe. Our mission is to help organizations
work smarter together.

COMPANY VALUES
- Transparency and trust
- Customer-first product design
- Inclusion and continuous learning
- Sustainable, flexible work

ORGANIZATION & TEAMS
SkyNixa is organized into the following departments:

1. Engineering — builds and maintains SkyNixa products (Platform, Mobile, DevOps)
2. Product — product management, UX research, and design
3. Sales & Customer Success — new business, account management, and support
4. Marketing — brand, content, demand generation, and events
5. People Operations (HR) — hiring, onboarding, benefits, and employee relations
6. Finance & Legal — accounting, payroll, contracts, and compliance
7. IT & Security — internal systems, help desk, and information security

New employees are assigned to a team within one of these departments on Day 1.
Your manager and onboarding buddy will introduce you to your immediate team.

REQUESTING TIME OFF (NEW EMPLOYEES)
All employees, including new hires, accrue PTO from their start date.
- New employees receive 15 PTO days per year, accrued monthly.
- Submit requests through the HR portal at least 2 weeks in advance.
- Your manager approves PTO requests.
- Questions: hr@skynixa.com or HR Help Desk ext. 100

See hr-policy.txt for full PTO, sick leave, and parental leave details.
