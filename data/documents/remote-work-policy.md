# SkyNixa Remote & Hybrid Work Policy

**Policy Owner:** People Operations  
**Last Revised:** January 2025

## Overview

SkyNixa supports flexible work arrangements to help employees balance productivity and well-being.
This policy applies to all full-time employees unless otherwise specified in their employment agreement.

## Work Arrangements

### Fully Remote
Available for roles designated as "Remote Eligible" in the job description.
Remote employees may work from any location within their country of employment.
International remote work requires VP-level approval and HR review.

### Hybrid
The default arrangement for most SkyNixa employees:
- **3 days in office** (Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday recommended)
- **2 days remote** per week
- Core collaboration hours: 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM local time

### Office-First
Some roles (facilities, lab, on-site support) require full-time office presence.
Managers will communicate this during hiring.

## Expectations for Remote Employees

1. Maintain a dedicated, quiet workspace with reliable internet (minimum 25 Mbps)
2. Be available on Slack during core hours with status set appropriately
3. Attend all mandatory meetings via video (camera on encouraged)
4. Travel to the office at least once per quarter for team events
5. Follow all IT security policies (VPN required for internal systems)

## Equipment

- SkyNixa provides a laptop, monitor (upon request), and necessary peripherals
- One-time **$500 home office stipend** for fully remote employees (receipts required)
- IT support is available remotely via the help desk (it-help@skynixa.com)

## Requesting a Change

To switch between remote, hybrid, or office arrangements:
1. Discuss with your manager
2. Submit a request via the HR portal → Work Arrangements
3. HR will review and respond within 5 business days

## Performance

Remote and hybrid employees are held to the same performance standards as in-office staff.
Productivity is measured by outcomes, not hours logged.
