SKYNIXA EMPLOYEE BENEFITS OVERVIEW
Plan Year: 2025

HEALTH INSURANCE
SkyNixa offers three medical plan options through BlueCross:
- Bronze Plan: Lower premium, higher deductible ($1,500 individual / $3,000 family deductible)
- Silver Plan: Balanced coverage ($750 / $1,500 deductible) — most popular option
- Gold Plan: Premium coverage ($250 / $500 deductible)
Company pays 80% of employee premiums and 60% of dependent premiums.
Enrollment window: within 30 days of hire or during annual open enrollment (November).

DENTAL & VISION
- Dental: 100% preventive coverage, 80% basic, 50% major (annual max $2,000)
- Vision: Annual eye exam covered, $200 allowance for frames/lenses every 2 years

RETIREMENT — 401(k)
- SkyNixa matches 100% of the first 4% of salary contributed
- Immediate vesting on employee contributions; company match vests over 3 years
- Managed through Fidelity — enroll at fidelity.com/skynixa

WELLNESS PROGRAM
- $50/month wellness stipend (gym, fitness apps, mental health apps)
- Free access to Headspace for meditation and sleep
- Annual biometric screening on-site (optional, incentivized with $100 HSA contribution)

LIFE & DISABILITY
- Company-paid life insurance: 1x annual salary (up to $500,000)
- Short-term disability: 60% salary for up to 12 weeks
- Long-term disability: 60% salary after 90-day waiting period
- Optional supplemental life insurance available at group rates

HEALTH SAVINGS ACCOUNT (HSA)
Available with Bronze and Silver high-deductible plans.
SkyNixa contributes $500/year (individual) or $1,000/year (family).

EMPLOYEE ASSISTANCE PROGRAM (EAP)
Free confidential counseling (up to 6 sessions per issue per year) for employees and dependents.
Call 1-800-555-0199 or visit skynixa.eaphelp.com
